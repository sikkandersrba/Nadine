Prioritization Dimensions

Four Dimensions of Risk Assessment

Nadine evaluates infrastructure risk across four interconnected dimensions — producing a defensible prioritization framework that aligns with governance standards.

01

Life-Safety Exposure

Systems whose failure could directly endanger building occupants, emergency responders, or adjacent communities receive the highest risk weight in all prioritization models.

Critical
02

Regulatory & Compliance Risk

Assets where deferred maintenance creates legal liability, code non-compliance, or insurance coverage risk are weighted for priority capital attention before enforcement action.

High
03

Operational Continuity Impact

Systems whose failure would disrupt essential public services, healthcare delivery, or institutional operations are prioritized for reinvestment ahead of purely cosmetic considerations.

High
04

Institutional Risk Tolerance

Capital recommendations are calibrated to each organization's documented risk tolerance, governance appetite, and public accountability obligations.
