Each sector presents unique governance requirements, funding structures, and risk profiles. Nadine brings deep sector expertise to every engagement.

Government & Public Sector

Federal, Provincial & Municipal Facilities

Government portfolios face the most stringent public accountability requirements. Nadine delivers FCA programs and capital plans structured for treasury board submissions, auditor general scrutiny, and public reporting obligations.

  • Treasury board and budget submission support
  • Audit-ready capital documentation
  • Portfolio-wide FCI benchmarking
  • Multi-jurisdiction coordination
Public Safety

Emergency Services & Justice Facilities

Police stations, fire halls, courthouses, and correctional facilities demand uninterrupted operational capacity. Our assessments prioritize life-safety systems and operational continuity above all other considerations.

  • Life-safety system prioritization
  • Operational continuity risk modeling
  • Critical infrastructure classification
  • Emergency system assessments
Healthcare

Hospitals & Long-Term Care Campuses

Healthcare infrastructure assessments require specialized expertise across complex building systems where failure consequences are measured in patient safety outcomes.

  • Medical gas and life-safety systems
  • Aging healthcare facility planning
  • Accreditation support documentation
  • Infection control considerations
Education

Schools, Colleges & Universities

Educational institutions manage large, aging building portfolios under significant funding pressure. Nadine's FCA programs are structured for school board and post-secondary governance frameworks.

  • Ministry of Education reporting alignment
  • Multi-campus portfolio management
  • School renewal program support
  • Accessibility compliance assessment
